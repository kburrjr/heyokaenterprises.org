Monetary Schemes & Systems

Designing value processing systems that are lawful, transparent, and grounded in fixed standards of weight and measure.

Areas of Focus

  • → Burden-Backed Value Systems — Tokenization of records and obligations, not speculative assets. Each unit backed by a provable burden declaration.
  • → Non-Custodial Clearing — Value processing where no party holds principal. Fees extracted atomically at transit. Zero counterparty risk.
  • → Payment Rail Architecture — Design of payment corridors using lawful instruments, postal money orders, ACH, and digital settlement systems anchored to trust frameworks.
  • → Fixed Weights & Measures — Application of Art. I, Sec. 8 weights and measures standards (avoirdupois pound, wine gallon, Winchester bushel) as the foundation of honest value.
  • → Accrual Accounting & Ledger Systems — Complete accrual-based accounting frameworks with running ledgers, daily witness attestation, and fiduciary closure.
  • → Tollbooth Economics — Tokenomic models where value flows produce a fee stream — provable, burnable, and usable as collateral for lending pools.
